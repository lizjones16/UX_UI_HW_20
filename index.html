A traveler/local connection hub that helps busy, overwhelmed travelers find authentic experiences wherever they go.

Research

Before starting this project, me and my partner did extensive research on the travel industry and where users felt most frustrated in the travel-planning process. Here are some of our responses:

James
“I want to understand what there is to do at a place that I’m going and that I’m not missing anything really important. How do I make sure I get the value I want out of my trip?”
Katie
“I’ve been burned by untrustworthy travel sites before. Unique travel experiences are hard to find, and I often find myself looking through several travel sites before I find a quality activity.”
Audrey
“The travel field is so large and overwhelming. There is so much sponsored content on those sites, and I hate doing touristy things but I often find myself sucked in anyways.”
The Problem

Travelers want to easily plan an authentic, non-touristy travel experience, but are overwhelmed with the amount of content on travel sites and skeptical of all the user reviews and recommended posts.

The Solution

A traveler/local connection hub that helps overwhelmed/skeptical travelers discover authentic travel experiences, by browsing through locals who can give honest tips on their area.

Iterations

After creating some initial wireframes and doing a few rounds of user testing on our prototypes, me and my partner found the following issues with our app:

  • The search bar on the main page needs to specify what it's searching for
  • The map needs to be labeled so it's clear where you are
  • The copy on the coaching screens needs to be refined
  • Info cards should be added to local guides on the map screen
  • Users want a list-view option for guides
